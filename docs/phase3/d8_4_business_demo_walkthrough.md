# D8.4 Business Demo Walkthrough

Status: READY_FOR_STAGING_HANDOFF

D8.4 turns the D8.3 runnable flow into a business demo that explains PartnerOS as an agent operating system for premium export brands. It does not enter D9, does not create proof records, does not validate real staging, and does not deploy or modify `service.intelli-opus.com`.

## Demo Story

Use `/demo-walkthrough` as the first screen for the walkthrough.

1. Customer development: start with a customer project or brand opportunity.
2. Product adaptation: map the requirement to HOSUN lifting systems or JOOBOO education furniture.
3. Quote: show that pricing and quote preparation remain operator-controlled.
4. Order: show the confirmed customer order as the internal source of truth.
5. Partner split: show HOSUN/JOOBOO as peer partner directions, not a hard-coded single-brand flow.
6. Production: show operator-maintained milestones.
7. Shipment: show manual logistics plans with no carrier API or automatic status mutation.
8. Portal: show customer-safe products, orders, production, shipment, resources, and feedback through the bridge contract.
9. Feedback: show internal triage with no auto-reply or SLA promise.
10. Market response: show how order demand, production friction, shipment risk, feedback, and watchlist data inform human-reviewed product focus.

## Scenario 1: HOSUN Lifting Systems

Position HOSUN as the lifting-system partner direction:

- Desk frames.
- Desk legs.
- Lifting columns.
- Heavy-duty lifting systems.

Demo value: PartnerOS keeps the quote, confirmed order, partner split, production milestone, shipment plan, customer Portal view, feedback, and market response in one operating loop.

## Scenario 2: JOOBOO Education Furniture

Position JOOBOO as the second partner direction:

- Education furniture.
- Classroom project furniture.
- Future partner expansion.

Demo value: the same PartnerOS loop works for a different product family and operating model, proving the system is not HOSUN-only or a generic CRM.

## Portal Operations Talking Point

Portal Operations proves the bridge value before real staging credentials are available:

- Products: customer-safe product fields.
- Orders: recent customer-visible order summaries.
- Production: customer-safe milestone summaries.
- Shipment: manual ETD, ETA, and tracking fields.
- Resources: customer-visible documents without backend paths.
- Feedback: customer intake to internal queue without automatic notification.

## Safety Boundary

The demo must not claim real staging validation. Keep the wording at `READY_FOR_STAGING_HANDOFF` until the real `PORTAL_CUSTOMER_API_TOKEN` and allowed origin for `service.intelli-opus.com` are supplied and verified.
