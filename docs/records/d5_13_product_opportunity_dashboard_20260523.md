# D5.13 Product Opportunity Dashboard & Quote Readiness Board

## Goal

把 D5.12 的单 lead product fit 升级成批量产品机会与报价前准备看板。

## Business Focus

- HOSUN lifting systems
- adjustable desk frames
- desk legs
- lifting columns
- JOOBOO education furniture
- medical / lab workspace
- project supply
- OEM / ODM components

## Workflow

1. 打开 Dashboard。
2. 查看 Product Opportunity Summary。
3. 进入 `/lead-intelligence`。
4. 查看 Product Opportunity Board。
5. 筛选 High Opportunity / Quote Ready / Lifting Systems。
6. 打开 lead。
7. 查看 Product Fit Card。
8. Copy Discovery Questions。
9. 人工外联确认缺失信息。
10. 设置 follow-up。

## Safety

- no automatic quote creation
- no price promise
- no inventory promise
- no certification promise
- no automatic sending

## Acceptance Criteria

- dashboard summary 可用
- board 可用
- filters 可用
- product focus 可见
- quote readiness 可见
- tests pass
- no DB migration
