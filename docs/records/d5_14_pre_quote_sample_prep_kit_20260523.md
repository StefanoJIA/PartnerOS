# D5.14 Pre-Quote & Sample Prep Kit

## Goal

把 product fit 与 quote readiness 转化为人工报价前/送样前的准备信息包。

## Business Focus

- HOSUN lifting systems
- adjustable desk frames
- desk legs
- lifting columns
- JOOBOO education furniture
- medical / lab workspace
- project supply
- OEM / ODM components

## Workflow

1. 打开 `/lead-intelligence`。
2. 选择 lead。
3. 查看 Product Fit Card。
4. 查看 Pre-Quote & Sample Prep。
5. 查看 missing quote info。
6. Copy customer questions。
7. 人工外联确认。
8. 记录 touchpoint。
9. 设置 follow-up。
10. 后续再由正式 Quote 模块处理报价。

## Safety

- no quote creation
- no pricing generated
- no inventory promise
- no certification promise
- no lead-time promise
- no automatic sending

## Acceptance Criteria

- pre-quote brief 可用
- sample brief 可用
- checklist 可见
- copy 可用
- tests pass
- no DB migration
