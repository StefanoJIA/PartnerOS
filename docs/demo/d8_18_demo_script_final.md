# D8.18 Final Partner-Facing Demo Script

## Demo Profile

- Length: 10-15 minutes
- Audience: HOSUN, JOOBOO, Chongqing Huiju, and future premium export-brand partners
- Goal: show PartnerOS as a Chinese-first internal operating system for multi-partner export agency work
- Current state: READY_FOR_STAGING_HANDOFF

## Opening

Use this opening:

PartnerOS is intelliOffice's internal source of truth. It connects customer development, campaign planning, manual outreach, quotation, order execution, partner split, supplier confirmation, production, shipment, customer Portal visibility, feedback, Market Response, and Partner Onboarding.

`service.intelli-opus.com` remains the customer-facing Portal. PartnerOS controls the internal facts. The Portal should only read customer-safe fields after real staging configuration is provided.

This demo is not a HOSUN-only workflow and not a single CRM. It shows a repeatable operating loop for HOSUN, JOOBOO, Chongqing Huiju, and future premium export-brand partners.

## 1. Workbench

Time: 1 minute.

Open `/`.

Say:

This is the daily entrance for the business team. The workbench separates the day into business development, delivery operations, and management decisions. From here, an operator can enter customer development, Growth Operations, quotes, orders, feedback, Market Response, Partner Onboarding, or Demo Walkthrough.

Show:

- Business development entry.
- Delivery operations entry.
- Management decision entry.
- Shortcut cards to Growth Operations, Quotes, Orders, Feedback, Market Response, Partner Onboarding, and Demo Walkthrough.

Transition:

Now we start where the business starts: customer development.

## 2. Customer Development

Time: 1 minute.

Open Companies, Contacts, and Lead Intelligence.

Say:

Before a quote exists, the team needs to know who the customer is, what industry they belong to, what region they serve, what product interest they have, and which partner direction fits. Customer development is where a raw opportunity becomes an operating lead.

Show:

- Company / contact context.
- Product interest.
- Partner fit.
- Lead Intelligence.

Transition:

Once the partner and product direction are clear, the team can plan a campaign.

## 3. Growth Operations / Campaign

Time: 2 minutes.

Open `/growth-operations`.

Say:

Growth Operations is the campaign workspace. It lets the team plan HOSUN and JOOBOO campaigns as peer partner directions, define product focus, segment, goal, owner, and next action, then connect later quotes, orders, feedback, shipment risks, and Market Response signals.

For HOSUN, the campaign can focus on lifting systems, desk frames, desk legs, lifting columns, and heavy-duty supply. For JOOBOO, it can focus on education furniture, school desks and chairs, and project furniture.

Show:

- Campaign list.
- HOSUN and JOOBOO peer positioning.
- Target segment.
- Product focus.
- Next action.
- Aggregation cards for quote, order, feedback, shipment risk, and market signal.

Safety line:

PartnerOS does not automatically send email, SMS, LinkedIn messages, or customer notifications. This is a manual operating workspace.

Transition:

Next we move from campaign planning to manual outreach.

## 4. Manual Outreach

Time: 1-2 minutes.

Open a campaign and show Manual Outreach.

Say:

The system can help prepare bilingual outreach drafts and follow-up tasks. The operator can update status, for example manual sent, replied, interested, or quote requested. But the system does not send anything automatically. The business person still decides what to send and when.

Show:

- Chinese draft.
- English draft.
- Follow-up task.
- Status update.
- No-auto-send statement.

Transition:

If the customer is interested, the next operating object is a quote.

## 5. Quote

Time: 1 minute.

Open `/quotes`.

Say:

Quotes are where product interest becomes a formal commercial conversation. The value is not only quote creation, but traceability: the quote can be understood in the context of campaign, partner direction, product focus, and later order conversion.

Show:

- Quote list.
- Quote safety boundary.
- Linkage to order opportunity.

Safety line:

PartnerOS does not automatically change quote status or expose internal pricing breakdown to customers.

Transition:

When a quote becomes a confirmed order, the operating responsibility expands.

## 6. Order Detail

Time: 2 minutes.

Open `/orders`, then one Order Detail page.

Say:

Order Detail is where the business loop becomes delivery execution. The operator can see the customer-visible summary, partner split, supplier confirmation, production progress, shipment plan, and feedback context in one place.

Show:

- Customer-visible summary.
- Partner split.
- Supplier confirmation.
- Production summary.
- Shipment summary.
- Feedback summary.
- Portal-safe fields.

Safety line:

PartnerOS does not automatically change order status to shipped or delivered. Operators maintain facts; customers see only safe fields through the Portal.

Transition:

Now we show how the same facts become customer-facing without exposing internal data.

## 7. Customer Portal Operations

Time: 1 minute.

Open Portal Operations.

Say:

PartnerOS is internal. The customer Portal is external. Portal Operations shows what can be customer-visible: recent orders, shipment status, feedback status, and market signal preview. It also keeps the forbidden-field safety boundary clear.

Show:

- Recent customer-visible orders.
- Shipment status.
- Feedback status.
- Market signal preview.
- Forbidden-field safety.

Safety line:

The Portal must not expose cost, margin, pricing breakdown, supplier private notes, backend paths, storage keys, or tokens.

Transition:

After delivery, customer feedback becomes the next operating input.

## 8. Feedback Tickets

Time: 1 minute.

Open `/feedback-tickets`.

Say:

Feedback is handled as an internal operating ticket. The team can update status, priority, owner, and resolution notes. PartnerOS does not auto-reply, notify customers, or promise SLA. Repeated feedback can become a Market Response signal.

Show:

- Feedback list.
- Detail drawer.
- Status / priority.
- Resolve / close.
- Internal-only handling message.

Transition:

Now we close the loop by turning operational signals into market judgment.

## 9. Market Response

Time: 1-2 minutes.

Open `/market-response`.

Say:

Market Response helps the team decide where to focus next. It explains signals from demand, quotes, orders, production friction, shipment risk, feedback, and watchlist gaps.

For HOSUN, signals may involve lifting systems, desk frames, desk legs, lifting columns, heavy-duty supply, project demand, load, stability, noise, or delivery feedback.

For JOOBOO, signals may involve education furniture, school desks and chairs, project furniture, school or institutional procurement, delivery coordination, and customer feedback.

Show:

- Signal source.
- Reason.
- Recommendation.
- Watchlist or partner focus.

Safety line:

Market Response is advisory. It does not automatically change quote, order, partner, or product status.

Transition:

Finally, we show how new partners enter the same operating system.

## 10. Partner Onboarding

Time: 1 minute.

Open `/partner-onboarding`.

Say:

Partner Onboarding proves the system is not HOSUN-only. HOSUN, JOOBOO, Chongqing Huiju, and future partners can all be represented as partner directions. Each partner can define readiness, product data, operating requirements, missing configuration, and next action.

Show:

- HOSUN readiness.
- JOOBOO readiness.
- Future partner path.
- Checklist.
- Next action.

Closing line:

PartnerOS lets intelliOffice operate multiple premium export brands through one repeatable loop: customer development, campaign, manual outreach, quote, order, production, shipment, Portal, feedback, Market Response, and partner onboarding.

The goal is to help HOSUN and intelliOffice understand not just who is interested, but which product direction is ready to scale.

## JOOBOO Specific Script

Use this paragraph when JOOBOO is the main audience:

For JOOBOO, the demo focuses on education furniture, school desks and chairs, and project furniture. Schools and institutions care about project timing, consistency, delivery coordination, and feedback after use. PartnerOS connects customer segment, campaign, manual outreach, quote, order, production, delivery, feedback, and Market Response so JOOBOO can see which project opportunities are real and what needs to improve before scaling.

A new partner can enter through Partner Onboarding, product setup, campaign planning, manual outreach, quote, order, delivery, feedback, and Market Response. The system keeps each partner's product focus and customer segment distinct, while giving intelliOffice one common operating loop for premium export-brand agency work.
