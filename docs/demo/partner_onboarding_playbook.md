# Partner Onboarding Playbook

Status: READY_FOR_STAGING_HANDOFF

Use this playbook when introducing a new premium export brand into PartnerOS.

## Positioning

PartnerOS is a multi-brand agent operating system. HOSUN lifting systems and JOOBOO education furniture are reference examples, not the only brands and not a ranking order.

Every partner enters as a peer manufacturing partner with its own product focus, market context, quote readiness, order readiness, Portal readiness, and demo narrative.

## Onboarding Steps

1. Create or verify the manufacturing partner profile.
2. Map product categories and product focus.
3. Add pricing basis or quote catalog readiness.
4. Confirm quote flow readiness.
5. Confirm order and partner split readiness.
6. Map production and manual shipment workflow.
7. Review customer-visible Portal fields.
8. Define market response focus.
9. Prepare demo narrative.

- Partner type.

- Product focus.
- Target market.
- Quote/pricing basis.
- Production and shipment operating path.
- Portal-visible field review owner.
- Demo narrative owner.

## Demo Use

During a partner-facing walkthrough:

- Open `/partner-onboarding`.
- Show that HOSUN and JOOBOO are reference samples.
- Show the future partner placeholder as the repeatable intake path.
- Open product catalog, Market Response, Orders, or Demo Walkthrough from the row links.
- Keep the current state at READY_FOR_STAGING_HANDOFF until real staging token/origin evidence is available.

## Safety

Do not use onboarding to:

- Enter D9.
- Add proof records.
- Claim staging validation.
- Send notifications.
- Trigger supplier/customer emails.
- Call carrier APIs.
- Commit `.env`, tokens, generated PDFs, `IE Auto.pdf`, `local_data/`, or `backend/storage/`.
