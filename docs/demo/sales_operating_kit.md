# PartnerOS Sales Operating Kit

Audience: intelliOffice operators, partner-facing sales leads, and implementation reviewers.

Current state: READY_FOR_STAGING_HANDOFF. This kit supports a business demo, not a claim of live staging validation.

## One-Sentence Positioning

PartnerOS is the internal operating system for premium export-brand agents: it connects customer development, product adaptation, quotation, confirmed orders, partner manufacturing coordination, production, logistics, customer Portal visibility, feedback, and market response.

## Recommended Demo Path

1. Open `/demo-walkthrough`.
2. Explain the full loop: customer development -> product adaptation -> quote -> order -> partner split -> production -> shipment -> Portal -> feedback -> market response.
3. Open the HOSUN lifting systems scenario.
4. Open the JOOBOO education furniture scenario.
5. Open Portal Operations and show how PartnerOS prepares the customer-safe Portal data contract.
6. Open a featured order and show the customer-visible operating summary.
7. Open Market Response and explain why the signals deserve operator review.

## HOSUN Talk Track

Use this scenario for adjustable workstation and lifting-system opportunities.

- Product line: desk frames, desk legs, lifting columns, heavy-duty lifting systems.
- Operator value: PartnerOS turns a customer requirement into a controlled quote, confirmed order, HOSUN partner split, production milestones, shipment plan, Portal status, feedback queue, and market response signal.
- Proof point: HOSUN appears as a peer partner direction in the multi-partner loop, not as a platform-only assumption.

## JOOBOO Talk Track

Use this scenario for education furniture and project-furniture opportunities.

- Product line: classroom furniture, project furniture, and future partner programs.
- Operator value: the same workflow supports a different partner category, showing that PartnerOS is a scalable agent operating system instead of a single-vendor CRM.
- Proof point: JOOBOO/future partner direction appears beside HOSUN in the demo flow.

## Portal Value Talk Track

The customer Portal bridge is valuable because customers can see filtered operating truth without entering the internal back office.

- Products: customer-safe product presentation.
- Orders: current status and customer-facing next step.
- Production: milestone progress without supplier private notes.
- Shipment: manual logistics status without carrier automation.
- Resources: customer-visible documents without storage paths.
- Feedback: intake into internal triage without auto-replies.

## Market Response Talk Track

Market Response is not just a report. It explains which product or partner direction deserves attention by combining:

- Quote and order demand.
- Customer feedback.
- Production delays or blocked milestones.
- Shipment risk.
- Product data gaps.
- Watchlist and focus-category signals.

All recommendations remain advisory and require human review.
