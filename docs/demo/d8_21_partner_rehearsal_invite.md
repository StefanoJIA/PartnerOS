# D8.21 Partner Rehearsal Invite

## Subject

PartnerOS rehearsal: customer development to order delivery, Portal visibility, feedback, and market response

## Invite Message

Hello,

We are preparing a short PartnerOS rehearsal for HOSUN, JOOBOO, Chongqing Huiju, and future premium export-brand partners.

PartnerOS is intelliOffice's internal source of truth for multi-partner agency operations. It is not a HOSUN-only tool and not a single CRM. The customer-facing Portal remains `service.intelli-opus.com`, which should read only customer-safe fields from PartnerOS after real staging credentials are provided and verified.
