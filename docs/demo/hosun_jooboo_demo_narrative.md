# HOSUN And JOOBOO Demo Narrative

Status: READY_FOR_STAGING_HANDOFF

This narrative supports the partner-facing walkthrough for HOSUN lifting systems and JOOBOO education furniture. It shows how PartnerOS supports multiple premium export brands through one operating loop.

## Shared Operating Loop

Both scenarios use the same PartnerOS chain:

1. Customer development.
2. Product adaptation.
3. Quote.
4. Confirmed order.
5. Partner split.
6. Supplier confirmation.
7. Production milestone.
8. Shipment plan.
9. Customer Portal status.
10. Feedback ticket.
11. Market response.

## HOSUN Lifting Systems

Product focus:

- Height-adjustable desk frames.
- Desk legs.
- Lifting columns.
- Heavy-duty lifting systems.

Demo story:

A customer expresses interest in adjustable workstation or heavy-duty lifting equipment. PartnerOS captures the product interest, helps the operator prepare a quote, converts the confirmed quote into an order, and assigns the relevant order lines to HOSUN.

Operational value:

- HOSUN receives a clear partner split.
- The operator tracks supplier confirmation.
- Production milestones show progress toward readiness.
- Shipment plans show method, ETD, ETA, and tracking metadata when available.
- The customer Portal can show safe order, production, shipment, and resource status.
- Customer feedback about ETD, lift-system specs, or delivery expectations becomes an internal action item.
- Repeated interest in desk frames, desk legs, lifting columns, and heavy-duty lifting systems becomes a Market Response signal.

Key message:

PartnerOS helps HOSUN turn product demand into coordinated delivery operations and market learning.

## JOOBOO Education Furniture

Product focus:

- Education furniture.
- Classroom tables.
- Project furniture.
- Institutional procurement programs.

Demo story:

A customer has a school or project-furniture requirement. PartnerOS maps that requirement to the JOOBOO product direction, supports quote preparation, converts the confirmed quote into an order, and coordinates the JOOBOO partner split through production, shipment, Portal visibility, and feedback.

Operational value:

- Project furniture requirements remain connected to the quote and order.
- Production status is visible to operators and customer-safe summaries.
- Shipment planning supports project-delivery coordination.
- Feedback about finish quality, classroom durability, or documents can be handled internally.
- Repeated education furniture signals inform future product resources and partner focus.

Key message:

PartnerOS is not limited to lifting systems. It can support project-based education furniture with the same operating structure.

## Why These Two Scenarios Matter

HOSUN and JOOBOO prove the multi-brand model:

- Different product categories.
- Different customer buying contexts.
- Different delivery concerns.
- One shared operating system.

This is the core PartnerOS value: intelliOffice can represent multiple premium export brands while maintaining one source of truth for customer development, orders, production, logistics, feedback, and market response.
