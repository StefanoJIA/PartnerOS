# Partner-Facing Demo Script

Status: READY_FOR_STAGING_HANDOFF

Audience: HOSUN, JOOBOO, Chongqing Huiju, and future premium export-brand partners.

Length: 10 to 15 minutes.

Purpose: show PartnerOS as the internal operating system for a multi-brand export agency, with `service.intelli-opus.com` remaining the customer-facing Portal.

## 0. Setup

Open `/demo-walkthrough`. Use the one-click walkthrough control panel as the main navigation surface.

Use this opening line:

PartnerOS is the internal source of truth for intelliOffice operations. It connects customer development, product adaptation, quotation, confirmed orders, partner manufacturing coordination, production, logistics, customer Portal visibility, feedback, and market response.

## 1. Position PartnerOS

Time: 1 minute.

Key message:

PartnerOS is not a single CRM and not a HOSUN-only workflow. It is a multi-brand agent operating system for premium export manufacturers.

Explain:

- PartnerOS holds internal operating truth.
- `service.intelli-opus.com` is the customer-facing Portal.
- The Portal reads only customer-safe fields.
- Operators stay in control of quotes, production, shipments, feedback, and partner response.

## 2. Open `/demo-walkthrough`

Time: 2 minutes.

Show:

- Customer development.
- Product adaptation.
- Quote.
- Order.
- Partner split.
- Production.
- Shipment.
- Customer Portal.
- Feedback.
- Market response.

Talk track:

This screen is the live map of the agent workflow. Each step links to the operational page that owns the work. The value is continuity: the same customer requirement stays connected from first interest through delivery feedback and market response.

## 3. HOSUN Lifting Systems Scenario

Time: 2 minutes.

Show HOSUN direction on `/demo-walkthrough`.

Explain:

- Product line: desk frames, desk legs, lifting columns, heavy-duty lifting systems.
- Customer need: adjustable workstation or heavy-duty lifting system program.
- PartnerOS flow: quote line -> confirmed order -> HOSUN partner split -> production milestone -> shipment plan -> Portal-visible status -> feedback -> market response.

Key message:

HOSUN can use PartnerOS to make lifting-system opportunities operational, not just visible in a CRM.

## 4. JOOBOO Education Furniture Scenario

Time: 2 minutes.

Show JOOBOO direction on `/demo-walkthrough`.

Explain:

- Product line: education furniture, classroom tables, project furniture.
- Customer need: school or institutional furniture project.
- PartnerOS flow: product fit -> quote -> order -> JOOBOO partner split -> production and delivery tracking -> customer feedback -> market learning.

Key message:

The same operating loop works for a different brand and product family. This proves PartnerOS is a scalable multi-partner agent platform.

## 5. Portal Operations

Time: 2 minutes.

Open Portal Operations from the control panel.

Show:

- Customer Portal operating value.
- Recent customer-visible orders.
- Shipment status.
- Feedback status.
- Market signal preview.
- Forbidden-field safety.

Talk track:

The Portal is not another internal back office. It is a filtered customer view of PartnerOS. Customers can see products, orders, production, logistics, resources, and feedback status without seeing internal cost, margin, supplier private notes, backend paths, or tokens.

## 6. Market Response

Time: 1 to 2 minutes.

Open Market Response.

Show:

- Why operators should care.
- Demand fit.
- Customer friction.
- Product readiness.
- Partner focus.

Talk track:

Market Response explains why a product or partner deserves attention. It combines quote and order demand, customer feedback, production friction, shipment risk, product data gaps, and watchlist signals. It is advisory only; operators still decide.

## 7. Featured Order Detail

Time: 2 minutes.

Open Featured Order Detail from `/demo-walkthrough`.

Show:

- Customer-visible operating summary.
- Current customer-facing step.
- Production summary.
- Shipment summary.
- Resource and feedback summary.

Talk track:

This page answers the customer question: where is my order now? It also gives operators one place to maintain the internal facts that power the customer Portal.

## 8. Feedback Tickets

Time: 1 to 2 minutes.

Open Feedback Tickets.

Show:

- Feedback operating loop.
- Ticket list.
- Detail drawer.
- Internal update.
- Resolve and close controls.

Talk track:

Feedback enters the internal operations queue. PartnerOS does not auto-reply, notify customers, or promise an SLA. Repeated feedback becomes a market response signal for product and partner review.
